Expand description

Creates a chain of strands to the player.

Each created Strand component has a dist of strand_dist, and a gravity of at strand_gravity.start near the player that slowly turns into strand_gravity.end. The function layers the sprites in each list of layer_groups based on their order, and creates an entity for each index in layer_group_order.
